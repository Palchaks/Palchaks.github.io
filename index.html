Anko software is best utilized on a data warehouse environment, post data upload from host systems. If your metrics are not yet on a cloud instance, we can assist in setting up a cloud instance for you as part of our product onboarding process.

  • The instance is wholly owned by you, ensuring complete control and security.
  • Anko seamlessly integrates with your cloud setup, enabling efficient data processing and analysis.
  • We have successfully provided this service to large mid-market customers, helping them transition smoothly to a cloud-based infrastructure.

Anko is a SaaS product that generates comprehensive knowledge graphs of your metrics and their relationships to other entities. These knowledge graphs serve as the brain of the language models, grounding them in truth. We can create these graphs from various data sources such as:

  • Data warehouses (Snowflake, GCP, Redshift, Databricks)
    •
  • BI Tools (Looker, Tableau, PowerBI, Thoughtspot)
    •
  • Modeling layers like dbT
    •

The knowledge graphs are fully mutable and can be easily edited to augment the model's intelligence, ensuring that it evolves with your business needs.

  • Once the knowledge graph is set up, our model conducts multi-dimensional deep root cause analysis whenever new data enters the schema.
  • It analyzes the data across various dimensions and makes detailed notes on the insights discovered at each Analysis Step.
  • The model then summarizes the most relevant insights and flags them to the respective owners or functions within your organization.

This automation saves significant time and resources, allowing your team to focus on strategic decision-making rather than manual data analysis.

Our SaaS platform conducts hundreds of root cause analysis runs—a compute-heavy process—and produces comprehensive digests in formats like PPT or PDF. These digests include:

  • Key Tables and Figures for all KPIs
  • Their variances from benchmarks and RCA of variance
  • Outliers in growth from benchmark and RCA of growth levers

This solution is perfect for systematic reporting in large organizations, where the first 80% of performance reporting can be completed with just one click. By automating this process, you can ensure timely and accurate reporting, enhancing transparency and facilitating better stakeholder communication.

The insights generated from automated root cause analysis and AI Digests are used by our model to distill actionable insights that should be immediately communicated via Slack.

  • The respective owners can review these insights on Slack, and for those deemed important, the model will ask relevant follow-up questions.
  • These questions can be edited and forwarded to the appropriate channels or business unit owners.
  • Any replies to these questions are tracked by the AI and fact-checked against future analyses, ensuring a continuous loop of improvement and accuracy.

Anko searches through unstructured information stores it has access to, such as Slack channels or email inboxes, to surface insights that it deems relevant to the data.

  • Insights posted to data stores (such as Slack, e-mail) are also taken into account, ensuring that as much relevant information is considered as possible.
  • Human-driven feedback decides the relevance of each insight given business context.
  • Replies to these questions are tracked by Anko over time, and fact-checked against future analyses, ensuring a continuous loop of improvement.

This solution delegates the job of tracking and validating actions taken translating to data insights by Anko.
